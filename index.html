About ALACRITY

As the name suggest Alacrity which bring words like full of enthusiasm, eagerness, happy & a joy full moments into the brains.

Alacrity is a festival celebrated by the AISSMS’s IOIT a 4 days set of events, plays, drama, workshops and much more. 3 Major pillar of these 4 days are Technical, Cultural & Sports. It changes the environment infrastructure of the AISSMS IOIT by the decoration, huge participation through out Maharashtra, a huge belt of stalls which create a mini market and boost the feel of festival like ‘Jatra of a Town’, It deals to exchange thought, idea, values between IOIT students & reality of market situation to create such a massive feast & turn into festival.
